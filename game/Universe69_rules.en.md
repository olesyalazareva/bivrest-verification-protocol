## 4. Preparation

1. Each player receives **6 Chances**
2. Players choose avatars (from the list or create their own)
3. A trump suit is determined (randomly)
4. The first player is determined (who has the lowest trump card)

---

## 5. Gameplay

### 5.1. Player's Turn

The player:
1. Plays one card
2. Performs the task from the perspective of their avatar
3. Group votes **"Believe"** / **"Don't Believe"**
4. If "Don't Believe" — the player pays **1 Chance** to the bank

### 5.2. Card Coverage

The next player must play a card that is:
- The **same suit**, OR
- The **same value**, OR
- A **Joker**

If unable, they buy a card from the bank for **1 Chance**.

### 5.3. Joker

The Joker breaks the rules. The player may:
- Stay silent
- Cover any card
- Do/Say/Ask anything

### 5.4. Forced Avatar Swap

**Special cards:**

| Card | Effect |
|:---|:---|
| **Six of Spades** | "Replace 1 participant's avatar and explain your choice" |
| **Queen of Clubs** | "Swap avatars of players to your left and right" |

### 5.5. Red Card (Stop Word)

Any player can say **"STOP"** and skip their turn without penalty.
**Limit:** 1 time per game.

- **Winner:** The player with the most Chances remaining
